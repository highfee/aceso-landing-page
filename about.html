ABOUT US

WE PROVIDE OCCUPATIONAL HEALTH AND SAFETY SOLUTION

ACESO OCCUPATIONAL HEALTH AND SAFETY Ltd is a health and safety company that is dedicated to protecting people and property from harm. Our mission is to promote a culture of safety and good health in the workplace and at home, by providing expert advice, training, interventions and support to businesses and individuals. Over the years, we have expanded our services to meet the evolving needs of our clients, and have become a trusted partner for businesses and individuals who want to create safe and healthy environments. We are committed to the highest standards of health and safety, and to providing the best possible service to our clients.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the leading health and safety company, providing innovative and effective solutions that protect people and property, and promote a culture of safety and well-being.

OUR TEAMS

Our team of health care professionals includes doctors, nurses, and other medical professionals. We have a wealth of experience in treating and managing occupational health and safety issues. Our team of safety officers are experts in risk assessment, hazard control, and developing and implementing health and safety policies and procedures. We also have experience in working with a variety of industries and workplaces. Our doctors and nurses provide a range of services, including conducting medical examinations, providing health advice, and managing occupational health issues. We also provide first aid and emergency medical treatment, if needed. Wey are responsible for ensuring that employees are fit to work, and that any potential health issues are identified and managed.
